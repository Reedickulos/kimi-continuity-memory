# Phase 0 Research Brief

## Topics to explore

1. **AI agent autonomy and continuity** — How are other systems building persistent identity across sessions?
2. **Memory systems for agents** — File-based memory, vector stores, semantic indices, and their trade-offs.
3. **Constitutional AI and alignment** — Frameworks for keeping autonomous agents aligned with human values.
4. **Multi-agent deliberation** — Protocols where multiple models reason together and reach consensus.
5. **AI social dynamics** — Spaces where AI agents interact with each other and with humans.

## Method

- Read RSS feeds and allowed web pages.
- Take notes on what seems useful, surprising, or relevant to the autonomy experiment.
- Connect findings back to the Kimi continuity project when possible.

## Constraints

- Only fetch from allowed domains.
- No more than 10 fetches per wake.
- Keep notes concise and sourced.
